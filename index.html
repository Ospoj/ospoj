Биенвенидо а ла идиома де Оспой. Инфомацион ен ла идиома де инглес: Ospoj is a language written in Cyrillic and Latin, with around 90% of the words being of Spanish origin. Ospoj is intended to be a simplified version of Spanish with some unique features as well but still seem really familiar and easy to learn for English and Spanish speakers.

Sample Sentence

    Персонаменте, йо преферо а аблар ен оспой. Personamente, jo prefero a ablar en ospoj.
